# Style Guide — Claude Code for the Rest of Us

## Concept

The visual language is inspired by **The Prestige (2006)** directed by Christopher Nolan.
Dark, theatrical, high-contrast. Things emerge from darkness. Restraint creates tension.
The aesthetic mirrors the content: mystery being revealed, magic being explained.

---

## Color Palette

| Role | Name | Hex |
|------|------|-----|
| Background | Near Black | `#080808` |
| Primary Text | Cream | `#F5F0E8` |
| Accent / Gold | Gold | `#C9A84C` |
| Dimmed Gold | Gold Dim | `#7A5A10` |
| Secondary Text | Grey | `#606060` |
| Terminal Green | Green | `#00CC44` |
| Terminal BG | Dark Terminal | `#0A1A0A` |
| Wax Seal | Deep Red | `#8B0000` |

**Rule:** Black dominates. Gold is the only accent. Never use white backgrounds.

---

## Typography

| Element | Font | Size | Style |
|---------|------|------|-------|
| Main titles | Georgia | 34 – 60pt | Bold, spaced |
| Pull quotes | Georgia | 26 – 42pt | Italic |
| Body / captions | Calibri | 13 – 19pt | Regular or Italic |
| Terminal text | Consolas | 12 – 14pt | Monospace |
| Attribution | Calibri | 13 – 16pt | Regular |

**Rule:** Never more than 5 words on a non-content slide. No bullet points. Ever.

---

## Layout Principles

- One idea per slide, maximum
- Every slide has a single visual element — no text-only slides
- High contrast between the one thing that matters and everything else
- Breathing room — never fill every inch
- Slow fade transitions only — no snappy animations

---

## Visual Motif

A **candle flame** appears on slides 1, 2, and 8.
- Slides 1 & 2: flame only, no hand visible (mystery)
- Slide 8: candle held by a hand (reveal)

This is the silent throughline of the presentation.
The same image, more revealed each time.

---

---

## What to Avoid

- White or grey backgrounds
- Bullet points of any kind
- More than one idea per slide
- Decorative lines under titles
- Busy imagery with multiple subjects
- Bright or saturated colors
- Snappy slide transitions
